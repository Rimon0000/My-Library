book details

3 A.M. by Nick Pirog is a nifty short story with a very peculiar slant. It is about a fellow named Henry Bins who suffers from Henry Bins syndrome. Two other people in the world have it. It's named after him as the first diagnosed with it. He sleeps 23 hours every day. He wakes up at 3 A.M. every day for an hour. He measures his time in minutes.
